The mission of the Fugue Foundation is to alleviate poverty by utilizing decentralized, distributed ledger technology (blockchain). We achieve this goal through two means: direct giving and education. First, we identify impoverished individuals through a vetting process and guidance from local charitable organizations while simultaneously working with community businesses to enable blockchain-based payment methods. As a result, donors are empowered to directly contribute cryptocurrencies (such as bitcoin and ether) to the well-being of the person in need. Second, we aspire to locate underprivileged individuals with a hunger for knowledge of emerging technologies and provide them with grants, tuition payments, and appropriate resources to receive training on open source, blockchain-oriented projects in an effort to have them gain lasting employment.

For instructions on how to interact with this decentralized application and how you can help develop this open source project, please visit our Github repository.
